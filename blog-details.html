Top 5 Places to See Sea Turtles

By Sarah Johnson | May 15, 2026

Sea turtles are among the most beloved creatures in the ocean. Their graceful movements and ancient lineage make every encounter special. Here are our top picks for where to swim with them.

1. The Great Barrier Reef, Australia

Home to six of the world's seven marine turtle species, the Great Barrier Reef is a turtle lover's paradise. Green and Loggerhead turtles are commonly spotted grazing on seagrass beds.

2. Sipadan, Malaysia

Known as the "Turtle Capital of the World," Sipadan offers almost guaranteed sightings. It's not uncommon to see 20-30 turtles on a single dive!

3. Galapagos Islands, Ecuador

The Galapagos Green Turtle is a subspecies unique to this archipelago. They are often seen resting on the sandy bottom or gliding through the currents.

"The best way to observe a sea turtle is to stay still and let them come to you. Never touch or chase them."

Conclusion

Whether you're a snorkeler or a diver, encountering a sea turtle is a magical experience. Remember to always respect their space and help protect their habitat by reducing plastic use.
