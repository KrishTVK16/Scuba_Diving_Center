Your ticket to the underwater world. The most popular diving course on the planet.

About This Course

The PADI Open Water Diver course is the world’s most popular scuba course, and has introduced millions of people to the adventurous diving lifestyle.

Get certified in just 3-4 days! We keep our groups small (max 4 students per instructor) to ensure you get personal attention and have fun while learning.

What You Will Learn

  • How to use basic scuba gear
  • How to breathe underwater (easy!)
  • Safety skills and buoyancy control
  • Marine life identification basics

Prerequisites

To enroll in the PADI Open Water Diver course or Junior Open Water Diver course, you must be:

  • 10 years or older
  • Able to swim
  • Medically fit for diving

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Usually 3 to 4 days. We are flexible and can work around your schedule.

Is equipment included?

Yes! All scuba equipment rental is included in the course price.

Can I learn online?

Yes, you can complete the theory portion via PADI eLearning before you arrive.
